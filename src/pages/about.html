About Me

Hi, my name is Darko.


I am a full stack developer in the making, currently undertaking the FlexTrack bootcamp at Coder Academy in Melbourne.


For the past 10 years I have been building a career in Supply Chain and Logistics, of which the last 6 years were spent in managerial roles where I was responsible for managing multiple, cross-functional teams.


The immense pace of the logistics sector, the large scale of operations and the diversity of the industry equipped me with a multitude of skills, and I am now looking to kick-start my career in the tech sector where I can combine the best from both worlds.


I will showcase my ongoing progress as a full stack developer in the Experience page, and you can ready about my thoughts and interests in the Blog section below.


If you would like to find out more about myself and my skills, please feel free to get in touch with me via my Contact page.
